# Daikin Service Center User Guide

This guide is for office staff who use the admin portal every day.

## 1. How to log in

1. Open the admin login page.
2. Enter your email address.
3. Enter your password.
4. Click **Sign in**.
5. If login fails, check the spelling of your email and password.

## 2. How to view new inquiries

1. Open **Inquiries** from the admin menu.
2. Look for inquiries with the **New** status.
3. Click **Quote** when you are ready to prepare a quotation.
4. Use the search box to find a customer name, reference number, or service type.

## 3. How to identify new customers

1. Open **Dashboard**, **Inquiries**, or **Customers**.
2. Look for the customer badges.
3. **NEW CUSTOMER** means the customer has no previous records.
4. **RETURNING CUSTOMER** means the customer already has past inquiries, quotations, or service records.
5. **MONTHLY SERVICE** means the customer has an active monthly service schedule.
6. **UPCOMING SERVICE** means the next service date is within 7 days.

## 4. How to create a manual inquiry

1. Open **Inquiries**.
2. Click **Manual inquiry**.
3. Enter the customer name, contact number, address, service type, aircon type, and concern.
4. Choose the correct status.
5. Click **Save inquiry**.

## 5. How to convert an inquiry into a quotation

1. Open **Inquiries**.
2. Find the inquiry.
3. Click **Quote**.
4. The quotation builder opens with the customer details filled in.
5. If a matching service template exists, the system loads the default items automatically.

## 6. How to use the quotation builder

1. Check the customer details.
2. Check the requested service type.
3. Review the labor and parts loaded from the template.
4. Add more labor or parts if needed.
5. Add notes or terms if needed.
6. Check the grand total.
7. Click **Save quotation**.

## 7. How to remove or edit labor or parts in a quotation

1. In **Quotation Builder**, go to **Labor & Parts**.
2. Edit the item name, quantity, or unit price directly.
3. Click the delete button beside an item to remove it.
4. The total updates automatically.

## 8. How to generate or download a quotation PDF

1. Open **Quotations**.
2. Find the quotation.
3. Click **PDF**.
4. The file downloads to your computer.

## 9. How to manage customers

1. Open **Customers**.
2. Search by name, contact number, email, or address.
3. Click **View** to open the customer profile.
4. Click **Edit** to update the customer details.
5. The customer profile shows inquiries, quotations, service history, and monthly service records.

## 10. How to create a monthly service schedule

1. Open a customer profile or open **Service Schedule**.
2. Click **Create Monthly Service** or **New service schedule**.
3. Select the customer.
4. Choose the service type.
5. Set recurrence to **Monthly**.
6. Choose the start date and next service date.
7. Add the assigned technician if known.
8. Click **Save schedule**.

## 11. How to mark a service as completed

1. Open **Service Schedule**.
2. Find the service card.
3. Click **Mark Completed**.
4. The system records today as the last service date.
5. For monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or annual service, the next service date is moved forward automatically.

## 12. How to reschedule a service

1. Open **Service Schedule**.
2. Find the service card.
3. Click **Reschedule**.
4. Choose the new next service date.
5. Click **Save date**.

## 13. How to check notifications

1. Look at the bell icon in the admin header.
2. A number means there are unread notifications.
3. Click the bell to open the notification list.
4. Click **Open** to go to the related customer, inquiry, or schedule.
5. Click the check icon or **Mark all read** when done.

## 14. How to update labor prices

1. Open **Labor Items**.
2. Find the labor item.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. Update the default price.
5. Save the item.
6. New quotations will use the new price. Existing quotations will not change.

## 15. How to update parts prices

1. Open **Parts Items**.
2. Find the part.
3. Click **Edit**.
4. Update the default price.
5. Save the item.
6. New quotations will use the new price. Existing quotations will not change.

## 16. How to edit service templates

1. Open **Service Templates**.
2. Select a template.
3. Add or remove labor and parts items.
4. Change default quantities if needed.
5. Make sure the service type match is correct.
6. Future quotations will use the updated template. Existing quotations will not change.

## 17. Common mistakes and what to do

1. **The wrong customer is selected.** Go back to the customer section and select the correct customer before saving.
2. **The quotation has the wrong item.** Remove the item and add the correct labor or part.
3. **The total looks wrong.** Check the quantity, unit price, and discount.
4. **A service still appears overdue after completion.** Refresh the page. If it still appears, check if the schedule was marked completed.
5. **No template loaded in the quotation builder.** Select a service template manually.
6. **A notification is already handled.** Click the check icon or **Mark all read**.
7. **The customer appears twice.** Use the existing customer record when creating a quotation or inquiry.
